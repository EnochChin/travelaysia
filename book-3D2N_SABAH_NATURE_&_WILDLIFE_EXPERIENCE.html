Package Overview

Immerse yourself in nature with our 3D2N Sabah Nature & Wildlife Experience, where untamed jungles meet extraordinary biodiversity. Sabah offers an unforgettable adventure for couples, families, and solo travelers seeking an enriching and exciting escape. This package is perfect for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers. From spotting orangutans to cruising along the Kinabatangan, Sabah offers a rich tapestry of natural wonders waiting to be explored. Book your adventure today!

Activities & Programs

Day 1:

  • Arrival in Sandakan and check-in at a serene jungle lodge.
  • Visit the Sepilok Orangutan Rehabilitation Centre and witness these majestic creatures up close.
  • Evening free to relax amidst the tranquil rainforest surroundings.

Day 2:

  • Morning river cruise along the Kinabatangan River for wildlife spotting—
    look out for pygmy elephants, proboscis monkeys, and hornbills.
  • Guided trek through Gomantong Cave to explore its fascinating ecosystems and
    learn about edible bird nests.
  • Evening river cruise for a chance to spot nocturnal wildlife.

Day 3:

  • Leisure morning to enjoy the lodge’s nature trails or birdwatching.
  • Check-out and transfer to the airport with cherished memories of Borneo.

Package Includes:

  • 2 nights’ stay at a nature lodge overlooking the rainforest.
  • Daily breakfast and one jungle-themed dinner.
  • Guided river cruises and cave trek with experienced naturalists.
  • Entry fees to Sepilok and Gomantong Cave.
  • Round-trip airport transfers and all transportation.
