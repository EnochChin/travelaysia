Package Overview

Unwind and relax with our 3D2N Langkawi Beach Escape, where turquoise waters meet soft sandy shores. Known as the Jewel of Kedah, Langkawi offers a perfect retreat for couples, families, and solo travelers seeking a tranquil yet adventurous getaway.

Activities & Programs

Day 1:

  • Arrival in Langkawi and check-in at a luxurious beachfront resort.
  • Enjoy a welcome drink while soaking in the sunset over the Andaman Sea.

Day 2:

  • Morning island-hopping tour, including visits to Pulau Dayang Bunting
    (Pregnant Maiden Island) and Pulau Beras Basah.
  • Explore Langkawi Sky Bridge and marvel at breathtaking panoramic views.
  • Evening free for shopping at the vibrant Cenang Night Market.

Day 3:

  • Morning free for leisure. Departure after hotel checkout.
