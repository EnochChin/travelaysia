Enoch Adrian Chin

Hello! I’m the creator of Travelaysia, and I’ve developed this website independently to provide an insightful travel guide to Malaysia. My inspiration comes from a belief that Malaysia deserves more recognition as an exceptional travel destination. Coming from Sabah, I’ve seen firsthand how tourism benefits our local communities, especially in villages where people can share their unique handcrafts, local delicacies, and rich cultural heritage with visitors.

This project is also a significant milestone for me. As my final-year assignment, it serves as a way to showcase the skills I’ve gained through 12 weeks of website development lessons. Every page, design choice, and interactive element represents my commitment to creating a resource that truly reflects Malaysia’s beauty and vibrancy.

Vision

To be the go-to digital guide that unlocks the authentic beauty and culture of Malaysia for travelers worldwide.

Mission

Travelaysia aims to promote Malaysian tourism by highlighting the diverse attractions across our cities, towns, and villages. We strive to educate visitors about Malaysia’s unique culture, empower local communities through tourism, and inspire travelers to explore the hidden gems and rich experiences our country offers.
